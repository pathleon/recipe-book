# Mojo verde recipe


## Ingredients

- fresh cilantro
- 4-5 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cumin
- olive oil
- white vinegar


## Instructions

- Grind the garlic, salt and comun on a mortar
- Add all ingredients to a blender bowl
- Blend as smooth as you want it
- Taste and adjust
- Happy eating!
