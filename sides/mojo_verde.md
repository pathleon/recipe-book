# Mojo verde recipe


## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch of fresh cilantro
- 4-5 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cumin
- 100 ml olive oil
- a drizzle of white vinegar


## Instructions

- Grind the garlic, salt and comun on a mortar
- Add all ingredients to a blender bowl
- Blend as smooth as you want it
- Taste and adjust
- Happy eating!
